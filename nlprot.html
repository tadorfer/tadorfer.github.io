NLProt

The application of Natural Language Processing (NLP) to protein sequences has recently gained traction in the field. There seem to be detectable parallels between spoken language and protein sequences, with sentences being analogs of amino acid sequences, and words corresponding to individual amino acids. This site is primarily for people interested in the application of NLP to proteins. The aim is to keep up-to-date with the recent literature in this field, so please feel free to make a pull request if you have come across some relevant work or feel like I have missed something.
